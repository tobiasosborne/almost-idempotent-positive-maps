The commutative scalar-module case: the [[def-jordan-coboundary]] over R^m on every one-dimensional
unital module (coordinate or Peirce-1/2 half-sum) has a norm-one support-unit right inverse, a bounded
projection onto coboundaries (constant <=3), and a dimension-free next-arrow estimate (constant <=12,
or <=2 for coordinate modules), including the intrinsic mixed Peirce-1/2 modules. Report
`prop:comm-scalar`; theory: agent-B/notes/commutative-scalar-module-splitting.md,
agent-B/notes/commutative-scalar-cocycle-projection-theorem.md.
