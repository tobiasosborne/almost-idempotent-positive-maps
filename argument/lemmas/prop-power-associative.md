Standard power-associativity of Jordan algebras: any single element generates an associative
subalgebra, which makes a functional calculus possible. Uses [[def-jordan-algebra]] and the
[[def-jordan-product]]. Cited background; report `prop:power-associative` (03-jordan-algebras.tex,
HOS Lemma 2.4.5).
