The qualitative cohomological input to the structure programme: vanishing of H^1 and H^2 makes the
Jordan correction step possible in principle (its size is the open quantitative issue). Uses
[[def-jordan-coboundary]]. Cited background; report `thm:whitehead` (09-structure-programme.tex).
