The exact (eta=0) Effros-Stormer theorem: a unital positive projection makes its range a genuine
JC-algebra under the projected product, the limit object the bridge approximates. Uses
[[def-positive-unital-map]], [[def-jc-algebra]], the [[def-jordan-product]], and
[[def-near-positive-projection]]. Cited background; report `thm:effros-stormer`
(07-exact-factorization.tex; Effros-Stormer 1979, Theorem 1.4 / Corollary 1.6).
