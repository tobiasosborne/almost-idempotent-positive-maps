The van Luijk-Wilming minimal sufficient Jordan algebra: an EXACT intersection of fixed-point sets of
unital positive maps, the qualitative template for the near-fixed-point algebra A=Im P (whose product
a•b=P(a∘b) is the approximate analogue of the exact Effros-Stormer product). Uses
[[def-multiplicative-domain]]. Cited background; report `rem:vlw` (11-discussion.tex; VLW Theorem
thm:luczak, introthm:K-generates).
